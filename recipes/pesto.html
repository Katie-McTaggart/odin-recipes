Pesto

Pesto is food from the Gods. Here is a recipe from Alejandra.

Ingredients

  • 5 bunches of basil
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • 2 cups of parmasean cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups walnuts
  • 1/2 Tbsp - 1 Tbsp salt

Directions

  1. In a food processor or other instrument blend basil, and oil.
  2. Roughly chop walnuts and cheese and blend these together. Do not run the food processor long if you like your pesto chunky.
  3. Mix basil and cheese paste together. Salt to taste. It will likely take more salt than you were expecting.
  4. Serve as desired

Pesto will have a strong bite initially. If you are not found of this simply wait 24 hours and the garlic will oxidize and decrease its bite.

Enjoy!
