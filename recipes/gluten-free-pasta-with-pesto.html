Gluten Free Pasta with Pesto

This is one of my favourite meals. Its delcious at anytime, but seems particularly a fall food.

Gluten free pasta comes in many varieties some the better options in my opinion are rice, amaranth, and cauliflower (if served immediately).

Ingredients

  • 454 Gluten Free Pasta
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Pesto

Directions

  1. Bring 4 L of water to a boil with 1 Tbsp salt and a couple Tbsps of olive oil.
  2. Add gluten free pasta to boiling water and lower stove temperature to medium.
  3. Cook pasta for the time required based the type, typically 8-10 minutes.
  4. Serve with pesto (see recipe here).

Enjoy!
