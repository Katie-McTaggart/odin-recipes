Mango Salad

What's better than mango salad in the summer? Not much! Give this recipe a try and enjoy it under the warm sun with a mojito.

Ingredients

Salad

  • 2 slightly unripe mangoes
  • 1/2 small red onion
  • 1 jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro

Dressing

  • 1 Tbsp fish sause
  • 1/2 of a lime
  • 2 tsps sugar

Directions

  1. Cut mangoes into 1/2 inch cubes.
  2. Finely chop red onions, jalapenos, and cilantro.
  3. Combine salad and place in refrigerator to chill for 1/2 an hour.
  4. Wisk together salad dressing ingredients, pour over salad and serve.

Enjoy!
